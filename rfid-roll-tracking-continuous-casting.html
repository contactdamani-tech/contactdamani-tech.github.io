RFID Roll Tracking for Continuous Casting Machines

Continuous casting machines contain hundreds of rolls exposed to extreme operating conditions including high temperatures, scale, water spray and mechanical stress. Reliable identification of these rolls is essential for maintenance tracking and lifecycle management.

Challenges of Roll Identification

In many steel plants, rolls are identified using engraved numbers or manual maintenance records. These methods are often unreliable and difficult to maintain over time.

  • Loss of roll identification
  • Incomplete maintenance history
  • Difficult inventory management
  • Limited traceability

RFID Technology for Roll Traceability

Industrial RFID technology allows reliable identification of rolls throughout their lifecycle. RFID tags integrated into rolls can be read automatically using industrial readers during installation, removal and maintenance operations.

Benefits for Steel Plants

  • Automatic roll identification
  • Improved maintenance tracking
  • Better lifecycle monitoring
  • Reduced operational risks
