Roll Tracking Systems for Steel Plants

Steel plants operate large fleets of rolls across continuous casting machines, rolling mills and maintenance workshops. Efficient roll tracking systems are essential for equipment management and maintenance optimization.

Roll Management Challenges

  • Large number of rolls in operation
  • Frequent maintenance operations
  • Complex logistics between workshops and production lines
  • Limited lifecycle visibility

Digital Roll Tracking Systems

Modern roll tracking systems combine identification technologies, maintenance databases and digital monitoring tools to provide complete visibility of roll lifecycle.

Benefits for Steel Plants

  • Improved equipment traceability
  • Optimized maintenance planning
  • Reduced operational risks
  • Better asset management
