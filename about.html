Industrial Sensor Engineering for Steel Plants

Who We Are

DAMANI Group develops high-performance industrial sensor solutions dedicated to steel plants and heavy industry. Our expertise focuses on harsh environment applications where reliability, durability and precision are critical.

Our Industrial Focus

We specialize in positioning and identification technologies designed for Coke oven Plant and continuous casting lines and demanding industrial processes. Our systems operate in extreme conditions including high temperatures, dust, vibration and mechanical stress.

Engineering Philosophy

Our approach combines industrial robustness with digital integration. Each solution is engineered to ensure long-term reliability, minimal maintenance requirements and seamless integration into existing plant management systems.

Our Solutions Portfolio

DAMANI Group currently commercializes two independent industrial solutions:

TRACKROLL-ID®

An advanced RFID-based roll traceability system enabling full lifecycle monitoring of rolls used in continuous casting operations.

VEROLINE®PI

A high-performance industrial position sensor designed for steel plants and harsh environments requiring precise and maintenance-free positioning.
