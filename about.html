Industrial Sensor Engineering for Steel Plants

Who We Are

DAMANI Group develops high-performance industrial sensors dedicated to steel plants and heavy industry. Our technologies are designed for harsh environments where reliability, robustness and positioning accuracy are critical.

Our solutions are used in demanding industrial installations including coke oven plants, continuous casting lines and heavy industrial machines operating under high temperature, dust and vibration conditions.

Our Facilities

DAMANI Group is established in the Rhône-Alpes region of France with headquarters located in Annecy, a commercial office based in Mâcon and production and logistics workshops distributed across several sites in Haute-Savoie.

These resources enable DAMANI Group to respond in a structured and professional way to the needs of both French and international industrial customers.

Industrial Expertise

We specialize in positioning and identification technologies designed for demanding industrial processes. Our systems operate reliably in extreme conditions including high temperatures, dust, vibration and severe mechanical constraints.

DAMANI Group technologies are widely used in positioning systems for coke oven plants, allowing accurate localization and synchronization of heavy machines such as charging cars, pusher machines, coke guide machines and locomotive units.

Engineering Philosophy

Our engineering approach combines industrial robustness with modern digital integration. Each solution is designed to ensure long-term reliability, reduced maintenance requirements and seamless integration into industrial automation and PLC control systems.

Our Solutions

DAMANI Group currently provides two industrial sensor solutions designed for harsh industrial environments:

Discover TRACKROLL-ID®

An industrial RFID roll traceability system designed for continuous casting machines, enabling full lifecycle monitoring and maintenance management of casting rolls.

Discover VEROLINE® PI

A high-performance positioning and identification sensor designed for harsh industrial environments including coke oven plants. VEROLINE sensors enable reliable machine positioning and synchronization in coke oven machinery.
