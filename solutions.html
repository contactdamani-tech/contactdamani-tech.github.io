Our Industrial Solutions

DAMANI Group solutions are designed to improve equipment traceability, positioning accuracy and operational reliability in harsh industrial environments.

TRACKROLL-ID®

TRACKROLL-ID® is an RFID-based traceability system developed for continuous casting machines in steel plants. It enables precise identification and lifecycle tracking of casting rolls used in extreme industrial environments.

  • RFID identification of continuous casting rolls
  • Lifecycle monitoring of industrial equipment
  • Improved maintenance management
  • Integration with plant maintenance systems

Discover TRACKROLL-ID®

VEROLINE®PI

VEROLINE®PI is an industrial positioning and identification sensor designed for heavy machinery operating in harsh environments such as coke oven plants and steel mills.

  • High precision positioning
  • Designed for harsh industrial environments
  • Reliable detection of positioning targets
  • Integration with PLC-based automation systems
