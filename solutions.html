Industrial Sensor Technologies

DAMANI Group designs industrial positioning and identification systems for heavy industry. Our technologies support steel producers, machine builders and automation integrators operating in demanding industrial environments.

TRACKROLL-ID®

TRACKROLL-ID® is an industrial RFID identification system designed for continuous casting roll traceability. The system enables automatic roll identification and lifecycle management in steel plant casting lines.

Reliable roll identification helps steel plants improve maintenance planning, reduce downtime and optimize production efficiency.

Discover TRACKROLL-ID®

VEROLINE® PI

VEROLINE® PI is a heavy-duty industrial positioning sensor designed for extreme operating environments such as coke oven machines, charging cars and pusher machines.

The technology provides reliable machine positioning even under high temperature, vibration and dust conditions typical of steel plant operations.
