Industrial Positioning & Identification Technology

VEROLINE®PI is an industrial positioning & identification sensor developed for applications where conventional sensors cannot operate reliably.

It is specifically designed for heavy machinery operating in environments characterized by high temperatures, vibrations, dust, fumes and mechanical stresses.

Harsh Industrial Environments

Industrial machines used in steel plants and heavy industries often operate under extreme conditions:

  • High ambient temperatures
  • Mechanical shocks and vibrations
  • Dust, fumes and corrosive environments
  • Continuous industrial operation

VEROLINE®PI provides reliable positioning & identification information even under these demanding conditions.

Typical Applications

  • Coke oven machines
  • Steel plant equipment
  • Heavy industrial machinery
  • Industrial automation systems

Industrial Benefits

  • High precision of positioning & identification
  • Designed for harsh environments
  • Reliable long-term operation
  • Integration with industrial automation systems
  • Reduced maintenance requirements
