VEROLINE® PI Industrial Positioning Sensor

Heavy-duty positioning and identification sensor designed for coke oven machines and harsh industrial environments.

VEROLINE® PI

VEROLINE® integrates positioning and identification functions for charging machines, coke guide machines and pushing machines operating around coke oven batteries.

Positioning is achieved using magnetic sensors while identification relies on RFID technology. This dual technology ensures reliable operation even in extreme environments including heat, dust and vibration.

Typical positioning accuracy is approximately ±3 mm.
