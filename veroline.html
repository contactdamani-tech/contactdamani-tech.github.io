VEROLINE® Industrial Sensors

Sensors designed for positioning and identification of machines operating in harsh coke plant environments.

VEROLINE® PI – Precision Positioning Sensor

VEROLINE® PI provides precise positioning and identification for coke oven charging machines, coke guide machines and pushing machines.

  • Magnetic positioning technology
  • RFID identification system
  • High immunity to dust, heat and vibration
  • Positioning accuracy ±3 mm

VEROLINE® I – Locomotive & Quench Car Identification

VEROLINE® I sensors are designed for identification, localization and synchronization of locomotive machines and quench cars operating in coke plants.

  • RFID identification system
  • Reliable machine localization
  • High resistance to harsh environments
  • Industrial design for coke plants
