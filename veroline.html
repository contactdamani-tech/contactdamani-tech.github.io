VEROLINE®PI

Industrial Position Sensor for Steel and Heavy Industry

VEROLINE®PI is a high-performance industrial position sensor designed for steel plants and heavy industrial applications. Engineered for extreme operating environments, it delivers reliable, precise and maintenance-free position measurement in harsh conditions.

Industrial Positioning and Identification for Harsh Environments

VEROLINE®PI is an advanced industrial solution designed to ensure precise positioning and automatic identification of critical machines operating in coke plants and steel production facilities.

The system combines high-precision magnetic positioning (≈ ±3 mm) with robust RFID identification, ensuring reliability in high-temperature, dusty and vibration-intensive environments.

Core Technologies

  • Magnetic positioning system for accurate machine alignment
  • Integrated RFID identification for automatic detection
  • Seamless integration with PLC and plant automation systems

Typical Applications

  • Charging car
  • Pusher car
  • Coke guide car
  • Quench car

Industrial Benefits

  • Improved positioning accuracy
  • Enhanced operational safety
  • Reduced unplanned downtime
  • Reliable performance in harsh environments

VEROLINE®PI is engineered to optimize machine performance, reduce mechanical stress and improve overall process reliability in coke plant operations.
