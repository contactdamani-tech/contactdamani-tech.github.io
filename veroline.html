VEROLINE® Industrial Sensors

Sensors designed for positioning and identification of machines operating in harsh coke plant environments.

VEROLINE® PI – Coke Oven Machine Positioning

VEROLINE® PI integrates positioning and identification functions for charging machines, coke guide machines and pushing machines operating around coke oven batteries.

Positioning is achieved using magnetic sensors while identification relies on RFID technology, ensuring reliable operation even under heat, dust and vibration.

Typical positioning accuracy is approximately ±3 mm.

Example of positioning ensured by the VEROLINE PI sensor mounted on the charging machine moving above PI targets installed for each coke oven. Positioning remains fast and precise regardless of environmental conditions such as dust, heat or vibration.

VEROLINE® I – Locomotive & Quench Car Identification

VEROLINE® I sensors are designed for identification, localization and synchronization of locomotive machines and quench cars operating in coke plants. RFID targets installed along the rail allow reliable machine identification even in harsh industrial environments.

Example of VEROLINE I installation used for locomotive machine identification and synchronization.

RFID identification targets installed along the rail allow reliable identification and localization of locomotives and quench cars.

Main Technical Data – VEROLINE® I

Reference : ELV2500.80

Reading range : 360 mm max

Reading field : 500 mm nominal

Reading speed : up to 8 reads/ms

Working range : 150 mm nominal

Power supply : 24 VDC ±10% / 2A

Serial interface : RS485A

RFID frequency : 125 kHz

User memory : 5 bytes / 40 bits

Rewrites : unlimited

Typical reading time : 69 ms

Data storage : EEPROM (>20 years)

Dimensions : H250 × W600 × D121 mm

Operating temperature : −20°C to +60°C

Protection : IP66

Weight : 5.98 kg

Compatible with HEAD I Ref. ELV0300.02

Compatible with HEAD PI Ref. ELV0600.04
