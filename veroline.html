VEROLINE®PI

Industrial positioning & identification sensor designed for heavy machinery operating in extreme industrial environments.

Industrial Positioning & Identification Technology

VEROLINE®PI sensor installed on a coke oven machine for reliable positioning and identification in harsh industrial environments.

VEROLINE®PI is an industrial positioning and identification sensor developed for applications where conventional sensors cannot operate reliably.

It is specifically designed for heavy machinery operating in environments characterized by high temperatures, vibrations, dust, fumes and mechanical stresses.

Harsh Industrial Environments

PI HEAD TAG target detected by the VEROLINE®PI sensor to ensure accurate positioning and identification.

  • High ambient temperatures
  • Mechanical shocks and vibrations
  • Dust, fumes and corrosive environments
  • Continuous industrial operation

VEROLINE®PI provides reliable positioning and identification information even under these demanding conditions.

Typical Applications

Typical integration of VEROLINE®PI in an industrial automation system with PLC for positioning and identification of heavy machinery.

  • Steel plant equipment
  • Heavy industrial machinery
  • Industrial automation systems
