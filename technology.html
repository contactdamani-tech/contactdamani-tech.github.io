Industrial Positioning, Identification and Traceability Technologies

DAMANI Group develops industrial technologies designed for steel plants and heavy industrial environments where reliable positioning, identification and traceability are essential for safe and efficient operations.

Our systems are engineered to operate in harsh environments characterized by high temperatures, dust, vibration and severe mechanical stress typically encountered in coke oven plants and steelmaking facilities.

VEROLINE® PI – Precise Positioning and Identification of Coke Oven Machines

VEROLINE PI technology is designed to provide precise positioning and reliable identification of coke oven machines operating along oven batteries.

The technology is used on the main service machines of coke oven plants:

  • Charging cars (enfourneuses)
  • Pusher machines (défourneuses)
  • Coke guide machines (guide-coke)

These machines must be positioned with extremely high precision in front of the correct coke oven in order to perform charging, pushing and guiding operations safely.

VEROLINE PI provides:

  • Very precise positioning of machines in front of coke ovens
  • Reliable identification of machines operating along the oven batteries
  • Accurate identification of oven positions

This combined positioning and identification capability ensures that each machine interacts with the correct oven during production cycles, preventing operational errors and improving process reliability.

VEROLINE® I – Identification and Localization of Coke Oven Locomotives

VEROLINE I technology is designed for the identification and localization of locomachines operating in coke oven plants.

Locomotives support operational logistics along the oven batteries and their movements must be controlled and monitored.

VEROLINE I enables:

  • Reliable identification of locomachines
  • Localization of locomotives along oven batteries
  • Monitoring and management of locomotive movements

This system helps operators maintain visibility of locomotive positions and manage traffic safely within coke oven facilities.

TRACKROLL-ID® – RFID Identification and Traceability of Continuous Casting Rolls

TRACKROLL-ID is an industrial RFID system designed for steel plants operating continuous casting machines.

Continuous casting machines use large numbers of rolls that guide and support the solidifying steel strand. Monitoring the lifecycle of these rolls is essential for maintenance and operational reliability.

TRACKROLL-ID provides:

  • RFID identification of continuous casting rolls
  • Traceability of rolls during operation
  • Historization of roll usage and maintenance events

This traceability enables steel plants to monitor roll wear, optimize maintenance planning and improve roll lifecycle management.
