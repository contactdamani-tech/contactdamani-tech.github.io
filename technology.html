Industrial Sensor Technologies for Steel Plants

DAMANI Group develops advanced industrial sensor technologies dedicated to harsh industrial environments such as steel plants and coke oven facilities. Our systems provide reliable positioning, identification and traceability solutions for critical industrial equipment operating under extreme conditions.

These technologies are designed to operate in environments characterized by high temperatures, dust, vibration and severe mechanical stress.

VEROLINE® PI – Positioning and Identification of Coke Oven Machines

VEROLINE® PI is an industrial positioning and identification sensor designed for coke oven machines operating along oven batteries.

Coke oven plants rely on several large service machines including charging cars, pusher machines and coke guide systems. These machines must be positioned with extremely high precision in front of the correct oven in order to perform safe and efficient production operations.

VEROLINE® PI technology provides:

  • Precise positioning of coke oven machines
  • Reliable identification of machines operating along oven batteries
  • Accurate alignment in front of coke ovens
  • Improved synchronization of industrial machine operations

These capabilities ensure that each machine interacts with the correct oven, preventing operational errors and improving process safety and reliability.

TRACKROLL-ID® – RFID Roll Identification for Continuous Casting

TRACKROLL-ID® is an industrial RFID identification system designed for roll traceability in continuous casting machines used in steel plants.

Continuous casting machines use large numbers of rolls exposed to severe thermal and mechanical stress. Efficient maintenance requires reliable identification and traceability of these rolls throughout their lifecycle.

TRACKROLL-ID® provides:

  • RFID identification of continuous casting rolls
  • Traceability of roll usage and maintenance history
  • Lifecycle monitoring of casting rolls
  • Improved maintenance planning for steel plants

This system enables steel plants to optimize roll lifecycle management, improve maintenance efficiency and increase operational reliability.
