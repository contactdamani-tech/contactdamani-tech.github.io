RFID and Identification Technologies for Steel Plants

Industrial identification technologies play an essential role in modern steel plants. Reliable identification of machine components allows operators to improve maintenance tracking, asset management and operational safety.

DAMANI Group develops identification technologies designed for the extreme operating conditions found in steel production environments, including high temperatures, dust, water spray and heavy mechanical loads.

Roll traceability technologies for continuous casting machines

Continuous casting machines contain hundreds of rolls operating under severe thermal and mechanical stress. Managing the lifecycle and maintenance history of these rolls is a critical challenge for steel producers.

RFID-based roll traceability technologies allow steel plants to track casting rolls throughout their lifecycle, from installation to maintenance and refurbishment operations.
