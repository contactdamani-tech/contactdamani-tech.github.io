Coke Oven Machines in Steel Plants

Coke oven plants are essential installations in integrated steel mills. Several specialized machines operate along the battery to perform different operations such as charging, door handling and coke pushing.

Typical machines include:

  • Charging cars
  • Pusher machines
  • Door handling machines
  • Quenching cars

Positioning Challenges

These machines operate in extremely harsh environments with high temperatures, dust, vibrations and aggressive gases.

Accurate positioning is essential for:

  • Precise alignment with oven chambers
  • Automation of charging and pushing operations
  • Safe operation of heavy machinery

VEROLINE®PI Positioning Solution
