VEROLINE® PI for Coke Oven Machines

Precise positioning and reliable identification of charging cars, pusher machines and coke guide systems operating along coke oven batteries.

Coke Oven Machine Positioning and Identification

Coke oven plants operate several large service machines moving continuously along oven batteries. These machines include charging cars, pusher machines and coke guide systems responsible for handling coke oven operations.

To ensure safe and efficient production cycles, these machines must be positioned with extremely high precision in front of the correct coke oven.

DAMANI Group developed the VEROLINE® PI technology to provide precise positioning and reliable identification of coke oven machines operating in harsh industrial environments.

Precise Positioning of Coke Oven Machines

Charging cars, pusher machines and coke guide systems must be accurately aligned in front of each oven before operations such as charging, pushing and coke guiding can take place.

VEROLINE® PI sensors enable precise positioning of these machines along oven batteries while ensuring reliable and repeatable positioning under extreme industrial conditions including heat, dust and vibration.

Reliable Identification of Machines and Oven Positions

In addition to positioning, VEROLINE® PI provides reliable identification of machines operating along coke oven batteries. This ensures that each machine interacts with the correct oven and prevents operational mistakes during production cycles.

The combination of precise positioning and reliable identification significantly improves operational safety and process reliability in coke oven plants.

Industrial Sensor Technology for Harsh Environments

VEROLINE® sensors are designed specifically for harsh industrial environments commonly found in coke oven plants. The technology is engineered to operate reliably despite high temperatures, dust, vibration and severe mechanical stress.

These sensors are integrated into industrial automation systems and PLC-based control architectures to support machine synchronization and automated operations.
