TRACKROLL-ID®

Industrial RFID traceability system designed for the identification and lifecycle tracking of continuous casting rolls in steel plants.

Continuous Casting Process

Continuous casting is a key steelmaking process that allows the continuous production of semi-finished steel products such as slabs, billets and blooms.

Severe Operating Conditions

Hot slabs pass through the roll segments under extreme thermal and mechanical conditions.

Challenges of Roll Management

Managing the lifecycle of hundreds of casting rolls is a major challenge for steel plants.

Traditional Roll Traceability

Many steel plants still rely on manual documentation to track roll lifecycle and maintenance operations.

TRACKROLL-ID® Concept

TRACKROLL-ID® is an RFID-based traceability solution designed specifically for continuous casting roll tracking.

Field Integration

RFID TRACKROLL-ID® Tags are installed directly in casting rolls using a dedicated installation method.

Software Platform

TRACKROLL-ID® includes several software interfaces for roll identification and lifecycle management.

Industrial Benefits

  • Digital roll identification
  • Complete lifecycle traceability
  • Improved maintenance planning
  • Roll lifespan analysis
  • Integration with ERP systems
