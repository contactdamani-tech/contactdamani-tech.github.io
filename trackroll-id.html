TRACKROLL-ID®

Industrial Roll Identification System for Steel Mills

TRACKROLL-ID® is an advanced RFID-based roll identification system developed for continuous casting lines in steel plants. It enables secure identification, traceability and lifecycle management of rolls operating in harsh industrial environments.

RFID Roll Traceability System for Continuous Casting

TRACKROLL-ID® is a complete industrial traceability solution designed for roll management in continuous casting lines. Engineered to withstand harsh environments such as high temperatures, dust, and heavy mechanical stress, it ensures accurate lifecycle monitoring from manufacturing to end-of-life.

System Overview

The solution is based on high-resistance RFID tags specifically designed for roll integration, combined with a robust reading infrastructure that collects operational data at every stage of the production and maintenance process.

Key Features

  • High-temperature resistant industrial RFID tags
  • Full digital lifecycle monitoring of rolls
  • ERP and maintenance system integration capability
  • Real-time production data acquisition

Industrial Benefits

  • Complete traceability of roll fleet
  • Improved maintenance reliability
  • Reduction of manual input errors
  • Data-driven operational decision support
