TRACKROLL-ID®

RFID Roll Identification for Continuous Casting

TRACKROLL-ID® is an industrial-grade RFID identification system designed for roll traceability in continuous casting environments.

It enables full life-cycle monitoring of rolls from manufacturing to maintenance and end-of-life.

Key Features

  • High-temperature resistant RFID tags
  • Real-time roll identification
  • Integration with plant ERP and maintenance systems
  • Improved asset management and predictive maintenance

Industrial Benefits

  • Reduced downtime
  • Improved operational transparency
  • Optimized maintenance planning
  • Lower total cost of ownership

Designed for Harsh Steel Plant Environments

TRACKROLL-ID® is specifically engineered for high-temperature, dusty and mechanically demanding industrial conditions.
