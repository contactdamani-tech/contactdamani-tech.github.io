TRACKROLL-ID®

Industrial RFID traceability system for continuous casting rolls in steel plants.

Continuous Casting Process

Continuous casting is a key steelmaking process used to produce slabs, billets and blooms.

A typical casting machine contains between 9 and 15 segments and more than 1000 rolls guiding the strand until full solidification.

Continuous casting roll segment
Casting segment structure with multiple rolls

Severe Operating Conditions

During casting operations, hot steel slabs pass between rolls under extreme thermal and mechanical conditions.

Roll identification systems must operate reliably despite high temperatures, scale and vibration.

Hot slab passing through casting rolls

Challenges of Roll Management

Managing the lifecycle of hundreds of rolls across maintenance workshops and storage yards is a major operational challenge for steel plants.

  • Roll installation on casting machines
  • Maintenance and refurbishment cycles
  • Storage management
Outdoor storage yard for casting rolls

TRACKROLL-ID® Concept

TRACKROLL-ID® is an RFID-based traceability system designed to track the lifecycle of continuous casting rolls.

  • TRACKROLL-ID® Tag – RFID tag integrated inside rolls
  • TRACKROLL-ID® Cross – identification tag for beams
  • TRACKROLL-ID® PDA – portable reader
  • TRACKROLL-ID® WiewApp – mobile interface
  • TRACKROLL-ID® myWiew – monitoring platform
