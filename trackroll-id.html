TRACKROLL-ID®

Industrial RFID traceability system designed for the identification and lifecycle tracking of continuous casting rolls in steel plants.

Continuous Casting Process

Continuous casting is a key steelmaking process used to produce slabs, billets and blooms. A typical casting machine contains between 9 and 15 segments and more than 1000 rolls guiding the strand until full solidification.

Severe Operating Conditions

During casting operations, hot steel slabs pass between rolls under extreme thermal and mechanical conditions. Roll identification systems must operate reliably in environments exposed to heat, scale, water spray and vibration.

Challenges of Roll Management

Casting rolls operate under severe industrial constraints and must be regularly inspected, refurbished and replaced. Managing this large fleet of equipment is a major challenge for steel plants.

  • Installed on the casting machine
  • Stored in maintenance workshops
  • Awaiting refurbishment
  • Being reconditioned
  • Scrapped

Traditional Roll Traceability

In many steel plants, roll traceability is still based on manual documentation or paper records, which often leads to incomplete lifecycle tracking.

TRACKROLL-ID® Concept

TRACKROLL-ID® is an industrial RFID traceability system designed to identify casting rolls and track their lifecycle across maintenance operations, storage yards and production lines.

The system includes several complementary components:

  • TRACKROLL-ID® Tag – RFID tag integrated inside casting rolls
  • TRACKROLL-ID® Cross – Identification tag for roll support beams
  • TRACKROLL-ID® PDA – Portable industrial reader
  • TRACKROLL-ID® WiewApp – Mobile application for field data entry
  • TRACKROLL-ID® myWiew – Web interface for monitoring and analysis

Field Integration

TRACKROLL-ID® tags are installed in dedicated housings machined into the roll structure according to controlled industrial procedures.

Software Interfaces

The TRACKROLL-ID® platform provides several software tools enabling roll identification, lifecycle monitoring and maintenance tracking.

Industrial Benefits

  • Digital roll identification
  • Precise roll location tracking
  • Complete lifecycle traceability
  • Maintenance optimization
  • Integration with plant information systems
  • Improved operational visibility
