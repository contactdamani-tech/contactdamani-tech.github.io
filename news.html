Latest developments from DAMANI Group including industrial sensor innovations, coke oven positioning systems and technologies used in steel plants and continuous casting lines.

VEROLINE® PI Sensor – Generation II

DAMANI Group introduces the new generation of the VEROLINE® PI industrial positioning sensor designed for coke oven machines and harsh steel plant environments.

The new generation improves reliability and positioning accuracy for charging machines, pusher machines and coke guide machines operating in coke oven plants.

TRACKROLL-ID® Roll Traceability System

DAMANI Group launches TRACKROLL-ID®, an industrial RFID system designed for roll traceability in continuous casting machines used in steel plants.

The system enables complete tracking of roll lifecycle, maintenance planning and automated identification of casting rolls.
