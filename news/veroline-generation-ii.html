VEROLINE® PI Sensor Generation II

DAMANI Group introduces the new generation of the VEROLINE® PI industrial positioning sensor, designed for high reliability in harsh industrial environments such as coke oven plants and steel production facilities.

Industrial Positioning for Harsh Environments

The VEROLINE® positioning system is engineered to provide precise machine positioning without mechanical contact. It ensures stable and reliable measurements even under extreme conditions including high temperatures, dust and vibration.

This technology is widely used for positioning industrial machines in steel plants and coke oven plants where operational reliability and safety are critical.

Typical Applications

  • Charging cars
  • Pusher machines
  • Coke guide cars
  • Quenching cars

Key Benefits

  • High precision positioning
  • Reliable operation in harsh environments
  • Maintenance-free industrial sensor technology
  • Integration with industrial automation systems
