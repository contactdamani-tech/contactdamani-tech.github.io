Continuous Casting Roll Maintenance Tracking

Continuous casting rolls are exposed to severe thermal and mechanical stresses. Monitoring their maintenance history is essential to prevent unexpected failures and optimize lifecycle management.

Maintenance Challenges

  • Thermal fatigue
  • Mechanical wear
  • Corrosion and scale
  • Frequent maintenance operations

Digital Maintenance Tracking

Digital maintenance tracking systems allow steel plants to record roll installation dates, refurbishment cycles and operational history.

Benefits

  • Better maintenance planning
  • Improved reliability of casting machines
  • Reduced maintenance costs

The TRACKROLL-ID® solution provides automated roll identification and lifecycle monitoring.
