Industrial Positioning Sensor Applications

High-precision positioning solutions for heavy industry

DAMANI Group designs and manufactures positioning sensors dedicated to extreme industrial environments. Our solutions are used worldwide in steel plants, coke oven plants and heavy machinery where reliability, accuracy and durability are critical.

Steel Industry Applications

Rolling mills and continuous casting

Precise positioning of moving parts is essential in rolling mills and continuous casting lines. DAMANI Group sensors ensure accurate machine alignment, stroke measurement and positioning control under high temperatures, dust and vibrations.

Handling equipment and cranes

Our sensors are used on ladle cranes, slab handling systems and steel transfer equipment, providing reliable feedback for automation and safety systems.

Coke Oven Plant Applications

Coke pushing machines

DAMANI Group positioning sensors are installed on coke pushing machines to ensure accurate travel positioning, repeatability and safe operation in extremely harsh thermal and dusty environments.

Quenching cars and charging cars

Position feedback is critical for quenching and charging operations. Our sensors provide stable and maintenance-free measurements despite heat, shock and contamination.

Heavy Machinery and OEM Applications

Custom integration for machine builders

DAMANI Group works closely with OEMs and system integrators to develop customized positioning solutions adapted to specific machine architectures and industrial constraints.
