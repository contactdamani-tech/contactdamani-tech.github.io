Industrial Applications

DAMANI Group sensor technologies are designed for positioning and identification applications in harsh industrial environments.

Steel Plants

Steel plants require highly reliable sensors capable of operating under severe thermal, mechanical and environmental constraints. DAMANI technologies are designed to provide accurate positioning and identification for heavy industrial equipment used in steel production.

Continuous Casting

Continuous casting machines include thousands of rolls operating under extreme conditions. TRACKROLL-ID® enables reliable roll identification and lifecycle tracking to improve maintenance management and production efficiency.

Coke Oven Plants

Coke oven machines operate in extremely harsh industrial environments. Reliable positioning sensors are essential for safe and efficient operation of charging cars, pusher machines and door handling equipment.
