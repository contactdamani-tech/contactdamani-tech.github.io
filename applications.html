Applications in extreme industrial environments

DAMANI Group position sensors are deployed in demanding industrial processes where reliability and accuracy are critical.

Sensors designed to operate where constraints are at their highest

Our sensors are used on continuous casting machines to monitor the position of mechanical components, segments and rolls exposed to extreme thermal and mechanical stresses.

Coke oven plants

In coke oven plants, our sensors operate in harsh environments characterized by heat, dust and vibrations, supporting reliable positioning and process control.

Machine builders & integrators

OEMs and system integrators integrate DAMANI Group sensors into their machines to ensure precise positioning and long-term operational stability.

Steel plants and steel mills

Positioning and monitoring of critical machines within steel production lines, including areas exposed to extreme thermal and mechanical constraints.

Continuous casting

Measurement and positioning at the level of roller segments supporting the passage of red-hot slabs, under extreme temperatures, heavy loads and continuous 24/7 operation.

Coke oven plants

Sensors adapted to high-temperature environments with dust and aggressive gases, ensuring reliable positioning and operation of coke production equipment.

Heavy industry and special machinery

Integration on custom-built industrial machines, lifting equipment, presses, conveyors and installations operating under severe conditions.

Thanks to a robust design and full control of its technology, DAMANI Group supports industrial operators and machine builders in improving reliability, safety and availability of their equipment.
