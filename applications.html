Industrial Applications

DAMANI Group technologies provide reliable positioning, identification and traceability solutions for harsh industrial environments including steel plants, coke oven machines and continuous casting equipment.

Steel Plants

Steel plants operate under extremely demanding industrial conditions including high temperatures, dust, vibrations and heavy mechanical loads.

Sensors used in these environments must provide reliable measurements and long-term stability while supporting industrial automation and machine synchronization.

DAMANI Group develops industrial positioning and identification sensors designed specifically for harsh steel plant environments and heavy industrial machinery.

Continuous Casting

Continuous casting machines include hundreds or thousands of rolls exposed to severe thermal and mechanical stress. Efficient maintenance requires reliable roll identification and traceability systems.

DAMANI's TRACKROLL-ID® technology provides industrial RFID identification of casting rolls and enables complete traceability throughout the roll lifecycle.

The system allows steel plants to monitor roll usage, improve maintenance planning and optimize roll lifecycle management.

Coke Oven Plants

Coke oven plants operate large service machines such as charging cars, pusher machines and coke guide systems moving along oven batteries.

These machines must be positioned with very high precision in front of the correct coke oven in order to ensure safe and synchronized operations.

DAMANI VEROLINE® technologies provide precise positioning and reliable identification of coke oven machines operating in harsh industrial environments including heat, dust and vibrations.
