Industrial Applications

DAMANI Group sensor technologies are designed for positioning and identification applications in harsh industrial environments.

Steel Plants

Steel plants operate under extremely demanding conditions including high temperatures, dust, vibrations and heavy mechanical loads. Sensors used in these environments must provide reliable measurements and long-term stability.

DAMANI Group develops industrial positioning and identification sensors designed specifically for harsh steel plant environments.

Continuous Casting

Continuous casting machines include hundreds or thousands of rolls exposed to severe thermal and mechanical stress. Efficient maintenance requires reliable roll identification and traceability systems.

TRACKROLL-ID® enables automatic roll identification using industrial RFID technology.

Discover TRACKROLL-ID®

Coke Oven Plants

Coke oven plants rely on heavy machines such as charging cars, pusher machines and coke guide systems. Accurate positioning sensors are essential to ensure safe and synchronized operation.

VEROLINE® sensors are designed for extreme industrial environments including high temperatures, dust and vibrations.

VEROLINE® for Coke Oven Machines

Heavy Industrial Machinery

DAMANI positioning and identification sensors are also used in heavy industrial machines requiring robust automation systems integrated with PLC-based industrial control architectures.
