Industrial Sensors for Steel Plants

Steel plants operate under extremely demanding conditions including high temperatures, dust, mechanical shocks and strong vibrations. Sensors used in these environments must provide reliable measurements and long-term stability to ensure safe and efficient production.

DAMANI Group develops positioning and identification sensor technologies specifically designed for harsh industrial environments. Our systems are used in steel plants to monitor equipment, improve maintenance strategies and support automation systems integrated with PLC-based control architectures.

Continuous Casting Roll Traceability

Continuous casting machines include large numbers of rolls operating under severe thermal and mechanical stress. Managing roll maintenance and replacement requires reliable identification and traceability systems.

The TRACKROLL-ID® system enables automatic identification of caster rolls using industrial RFID technology. This solution improves roll lifecycle tracking, optimizes maintenance planning and reduces unplanned production stops.

Coke Oven Machine Positioning

Coke oven plants rely on heavy machines such as charging cars, pusher machines and coke guide systems. Accurate positioning sensors are essential to ensure synchronization and safe operation of these machines.

VEROLINE® positioning sensors are designed for the extreme environmental conditions found in coke oven plants, including high temperatures, dust and mechanical vibrations. These sensors provide reliable positioning data used by industrial automation systems.
