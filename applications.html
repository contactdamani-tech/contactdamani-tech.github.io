Industrial Applications

Damani Group position sensors are deployed in demanding industrial processes where reliability and accuracy are critical.

Steel plants & continuous casting

Our sensors are used on continuous casting machines to monitor the position of mechanical components, segments and rolls exposed to extreme thermal and mechanical stresses.

Coke oven plants

In coke oven plants, our sensors operate in harsh environments characterized by heat, dust and vibrations, supporting reliable positioning and process control.

Machine builders & integrators

OEMs and system integrators integrate Damani Group sensors into their machines to ensure precise positioning and long-term operational stability.
