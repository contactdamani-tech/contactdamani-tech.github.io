Industrial Positioning and Identification Sensor Applications

High-precision positioning and identification solutions for heavy industry

DAMANI Group designs and manufactures positioning and identification sensors dedicated to extreme industrial environments. Our technologies are used worldwide in steel plants, coke oven plants and heavy machinery where reliability, accuracy and durability are critical.

Industrial applications requiring reliable positioning

DAMANI Group sensors are used in critical industrial applications where precise positioning, identification and operational reliability are essential. Our technologies support production continuity, equipment safety and process efficiency in demanding environments.

Typical applications include steel plants, continuous casting lines, coke oven plants, heavy machinery and automated industrial systems where robustness and long-term stability are key requirements.

Steel Industry Applications

Rolling Mills and Continuous Casting

Precise identification of moving parts is essential in rolling mills and continuous casting lines. DAMANI Group sensors ensure accurate machine alignment, stroke measurement and identification control under high temperatures, dust and vibrations typical of steel production environments.

Steel Plants

Accurate positioning of industrial equipment in steel plants improves reliability, safety and process optimization. Our sensors provide robust position feedback for automated systems used in steel manufacturing operations.

Continuous Casting Machines

Continuous casting machines require extremely reliable positioning systems. DAMANI Group sensors are designed to operate in environments with high temperatures, vibrations and intense mechanical stress.

These solutions are often combined with technologies such as TRACKROLL-ID® roll traceability systems to improve monitoring and process control in continuous casting lines.

Coke Oven Plants

Coke oven plants represent some of the most demanding industrial environments. DAMANI Group positioning technologies support machine automation, safety and operational reliability for coke plant equipment.

Coke Oven Machine Applications

Our sensors are installed on critical coke oven machinery including:

  • Charging cars
  • Pusher machines
  • Coke guide machines
  • Quenching cars

These systems provide stable and maintenance-free positioning measurements despite extreme heat, dust and mechanical shocks.

Many coke oven applications use VEROLINE®PI positioning sensors for high-precision machine positioning.

Heavy Machinery and OEM Applications

Custom Integration for Machine Builders

DAMANI Group works closely with OEMs and system integrators to develop customized positioning solutions adapted to specific machine architectures and industrial constraints.

Our sensors are used for steel transfer equipment and automated heavy machinery, providing reliable feedback for industrial automation and safety systems.
