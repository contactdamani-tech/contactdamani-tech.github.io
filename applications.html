Industrial Positioning and Identification Sensor Applications

High-precision positioning and Identification solutions for heavy industry

DAMANI Group designs and manufactures positioning and Identification sensors dedicated to extreme industrial environments. Our solutions are used worldwide in steel plants, coke oven plants and heavy machinery where reliability, accuracy and durability are critical.

Industrial applications requiring reliable positioning

DAMANI Group sensors are used in critical industrial applications where precise positioning, Identification and operational reliability are essential. Our solutions support production continuity, equipment safety and process efficiency in demanding environments.

Typical applications include steel plants, continuous casting lines, coke oven plants, heavy machinery and automated industrial systems, where robustness and long-term stability are key requirements.

Steel Industry Applications

Rolling mills and continuous casting

Precise Identification of moving parts is essential in rolling mills and continuous casting lines. DAMANI Group sensors ensure accurate machine alignment, stroke measurement and Identification control under high temperatures, dust and vibrations.

Steel Plants

Accurate positioning of industrial equipment in steel plants, ensuring reliability, safety and process optimization.

Continuous Casting Machines

Robust sensors for roll positioning and machine movements in continuous casting environments with high temperatures and vibrations.

Coke Oven Plants

Position monitoring solutions adapted to harsh coke oven conditions, dust, heat and mechanical stress.

Heavy Industry & OEM Machinery

Custom positioning solutions for machine builders, integrators and heavy industry applications.

Coke Oven Plant Applications

Coke pushing machines, charging machine, guide coke machines

DAMANI Group positioning and Identification sensors are installed on coke pushing machines, charging machine, guide coke machines to ensure accurate travel positioning, repeatability and safe operation in extremely harsh thermal and dusty environments.

Quenching cars

Position feedback is critical for coke machines operations. Our sensors provide stable and maintenance-free measurements despite heat, shock and contamination.
